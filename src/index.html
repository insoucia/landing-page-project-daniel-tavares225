Hopeless? Well...

It has been many years since the civilization of human beings, who had been extremely prosperous, collapsed, leaving a world where most of the creatures have died and everything is over. All that remains is a ruined megacity and decaying machinery. When did the world end, why did the world end? Chito and Yuri, riding around on their Kettenkrad, ponder these and many other questions as they wander this lifeless world of ruins. Set in a world where everything has come to an end The doomsday fantasy of two girls traveling is about to begin.

Meet the Girls

Chito

Chito or "Chi" is a soft-spoken, quiet girl. She is responsible for the much of the organization and record-keeping for the duo, as well as serving as the main driver of the Kettenkrad. Although she can not read much of the text of the older world, Chi loves books and collects them whenever she reasonably can.

Yuuri

Yuuri or "Yuu" is an intensely curious girl who just follows her gut, literally. She loves food intensely and is always on the lookout for more. The Marksman of the pair, Yuu can always be relied about, despite her outwardly distracted behavior.
